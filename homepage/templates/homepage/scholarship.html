2025 Latinos in Finance (LIF) Scholarship Application

Welcome to the Latinos in Finance 2025 Scholarship Application!



The Latinos in Finance (LIF) Scholarship is dedicated to supporting students in their academic pursuits by alleviating the financial burden of higher education. Our mission is to develop the next generation of Latinx leaders and foster life-changing opportunities by building a diverse and inclusive career pipeline into the financial industry.


Thanks to the generous contributions of our corporate sponsors, Latinos in Finance is proud to award multiple scholarships to outstanding students who meet the specified criteria. Scholarship recipients may receive awards of up to $1,500, as determined by the selection committee.


BASIC ELIGIBILITY

To be eligible for the Latinos in Finance (LIF) Scholarship, applicants must:

  • Be enrolled in a Bachelor’s degree program at a U.S.-accredited, not-for-profit, private, or public college or university. Students pursuing or completing their Associate’s degree or fulfilling general education requirements for a Bachelor’s program at a community college are also encouraged to apply.
  • Have a minimum cumulative weighted GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale.
  • Be available to attend the 2025 LIF Bootcamp (in person or virtually) in September and provide required materials for future marketing campaigns. *

*Follow us on social media for the official save-the-date announcement: LinkedIn | Facebook | Instagram


Ideal Candidate

  • Outstanding academic achievement
  • Strong leadership abilities
  • Exceptional personal and professional success skills
  • A keen interest in Finance, Mathematics, Statistics, Business, Economics, or Accounting

Application Deadline

The deadline to submit this application is: Friday, August 22, 2025, at 11:59 PM PST

This scholarship is needs-based financial aid, and we encourage all eligible students to apply.

If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out at scholarships@latinosinfinance.org.

Take the next step in your academic and professional journey—apply today!
